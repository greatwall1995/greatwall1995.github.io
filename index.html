  • Expert Student of Algorithmic Game Theory (Spring 2017).

    I took Algorithmic Game Theory in spring 2017 which was given by Prof. Xiaotie Deng. I took the role of the expert student in Nash Equilibrium section. My job was to propose a problem for the midterm exam. I designed the question carefully to cover all knowledge in Nash Equilibrium section. Here are the problem and solution.

    I further studied an open problem given by the teacher. During the study, I proved that for any two natural numbers m and n, there exists a bimatrix has m pure strategy Nash equilibrium and n mixed strategy Nash equilibrium. Here is the proof.

  • Part-time Teacher of Design and Analysis of Algorithms (Fall 2016).

    I was invited to teach algorithms for senior high school students in Shanghai Kongjiang High School. I taught a variety of basic algorithms including traversing method, divide and conquer, graph theory, dynamic programming, and number theory. I also introduced practical data structure such as stack, queue, merge-find set, heap, segment tree, hash table and trie. About one-third students in my class won first prize in National Olympiad in Informatics in the Provinces 2016. Here is the course site.

  • Teaching Assistant of CS122: Introduction to Programming (Fall 2015).

    I was selected as teaching assistant of CS122: Introduction to Programming. My job was to prepare the problems of tests and assignments. I also conducted a one-on-one meeting to help students with forming good programming style habits. Here is the course site.

  • Problem Setter of Kongjiang Cup (2013, 2015, 2016).

    Kongjiang Cup is a programming contest for high school students in Yangpu District, Shanghai. My job was to propose problems for junior high school students. I had to prepare four or five problems for each contest.
