Tone Classifier

Tone Classifier is contributed by Cheng Wan, Xiaojun Xu and Jiaheng Zhang. In this project, we design a deep learning model for recognizing the tone of the voice signal of Chinese words. We further develop sophisticated feature extraction method and denoising mechanism. The classification accuracy achieves 97% while the baseline is 88% and the highest accuracy is 98%.

We also conduct an empirical comparison between several deep learning toolkits (MXNet, CNTK and Theano) based on this project.

In this work, I designed the feature extraction method and denoising mechanism independently and implemented the code of MXNet version.
