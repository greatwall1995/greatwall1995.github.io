BadKid

BadKid is a group work given by Songyu Ke, Cheng Wan, Runzhe Yang and Tianyao Chen. It is a virus which infects ELF files. The general idea is as follows. To infect a new ELF file, the virus will construct a wrapper which contains both the virus and the target file. For the new file, during its running time, it will run the virus first then run its original code.

To simulate this mechanism, we first compile our virus as a dynamic library. During the process of infecting a new file, the virus just needs to change the head of the file and modify .dynstr segment and .dynamic segment.

In this project, my job is to explore the structure of EFL files independently and design the infection method with my teammates.
